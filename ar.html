I started working with augmented reality experiences for Instagram, Facebook and Snapchat in 2019. In addition to the implementation of the effects of various complexity (from conventional filters with retouching and color correction to complex 3D scenes and games), I also provide private advisory service and teach the art of creation of AR experiences across several online education platforms including Netology.
Custom assets of any complexity for the effects are always created by moi and Katya Tolcheva who is in charge of 2D elements.

Superposition LP
art direction, collage, print, lurmish
2024
For longtime collaborators Lurmish, I art directed and designed the cover for Superposition — an analogue interpretation of the fundamental physics principle stating that physical solutions of linear systems are linear. Blending mixed-media collage with repurposed Hi8 footage, the artwork captures multiple states at once, culminating in a composition that gives a subtle nod to Da Vinci’s Vitruvian form. Surreal yet tactile, raw yet precise — just like the music itself.

Music Factory
identity, art direction, social media, motion, print, merch
2024
For two years, I shaped the visual identity of Music Factory, a residency program fostering Russia’s next wave of musicians. Built on two pillars — music as the core and factory as the structured creative process — the branding adapted for each iteration with distinct color palettes and evolving graphic elements, all tied together by a cohesive visual system. Originally crafted in 2023 and refined in 2024, the design covered everything from social media and livestream graphics to promotional materials, merch, and event visuals. Over its run, Music Factory attracted 2,000+ applicants, reached over 50 million on social media, and helped 196 residents release 78 songs, amassing 3.5 million streams.

Golosa Live Performance Backdrop
art direction, motion
2024
For Golosa, I created a backdrop that visually captured the song’s theme of rediscovering one's voice. Starting with an original photo of Bernshtein, I crafted a frozen version and animated the transition within a digital room I built, where I relit the scene to emphasize the gradual melting of the ice statue. This process symbolizes her journey from silence to self-expression — reflecting the song’s metaphor of emerging from a cold, enduring winter into a spring of awakening.

Hypnos. Egress LP

Hypnos Era Promo
art direction, social media, collage, 3d, print, merch, photo, lurmish
2021
Expanding on the Hypnos LP visuals, Lurmish and I developed a promotional photoshoot that hinted at Melusine’s presence—close but never fully seen. Her tail coils around Karina and Arseniy, a quiet reminder of her myth, while in other shots, we explored water control and her elusive powers. Together, we sketched the initial concepts, I shot the photos, and then we brought the imagery to life—integrating 3D elements and compositing to create an eerie, dreamlike atmosphere. These visuals, along with custom 3D objects, became the backbone of the band's press pack, posters, flyers, merch, and other promotional materials.

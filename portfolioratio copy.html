Holod Remix EP
art direction, collage, lurmish
2025
A chilling exploration of loneliness and the freezing feeling of being lost in pain. For the Holod Remix EP, I reimagined the artwork from the Superposition album, expanding on its theme of overlapping elements. The new visuals explore this concept from a digital standpoint, merging emotion and technology. Stay tuned for the upcoming release.

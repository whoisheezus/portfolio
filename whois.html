WHO

HEEZUS?

My name is Nikita Myshkin and I’m a digital artist working in the fields of photography, 3D and AR.

I graduated from ETU LETI with a degree in Communication Technologies and Communication Systems and it really had a strong impact on how my work process is organized and what methods I use when creating a product. At the same time, I am a big supporter of abstract art and non-linear storytelling, so the result of the workings of my mind is often a good mix of theoretical foundation and surrealism.

Oh, and sometimes I also mess with designing websites. For example, this portfolio you’re browsing through was created and coded by yours truly.

In other words, I take the best of both worlds being an engineer by training and an artist by vocation and you can always call me an imagineer. c:

My current CV can be found here and I am available for new commissions and job offers. Feel free to contact me via nico@heez.us or telegram and follow on instagram or twitter.

hypnos artworks and promotional materials

The goal of this project was to create stunning visuals and artworks for 'Hypnos', the sophomore album of Lurmish. 3D was implemented to depict Karina, the lead vocalist of the band, as a Melusine, a female spirit of fresh water. In collaboration with Lurmish we created cg assets - tail, scales, bra and water flows - to compose them into the real photos taken by yours truly.
